About {{ ctf_name }}

TechnoVIT's Flagship CTF competition.

Step into Chronopolis, where time loops and shadows collide. Unravel the mysteries of time manipulation as you unlock new flag. This CTF event features challenges that may lead you down different paths with our unique multi-flag mechanic. Some CTFs may have more than one flag which unlocks new challenges and story elements. Choose your path wisely, as each decision shapes your journey through the temporal maze.

  1. Register: Create an account with your username, Full name, password and email. This is final and CANNOT BE CHANGED later
  2. ACT system: Start out with ACT I and progress through the acts by solving challenges from different categories of challenges.
  3. Solve: Download challenge files and solve locally and submit the flag. Or start challenge containers (on supported challenges) and find the unique flag for your Team
  4. Score: Parabolic dynamic scoring mechanic may adjust values as solves increase. Your points reflect the current value of the challenges. You DO NOT keep the same amount of points you had while submitting the flag

This CTF uses dynamic scoring. Challenge values decrease as more teams solve them. Your points reflect the current value of the challenges. You DO NOT keep the same amount of points you had while submitting the flag.

  • Do not attack the CTF infrastructure. Any attempt will result in disqualification.
  • Do not share flags or solutions with other teams. We take this seriously and violations will result in penalties/disqualification.
  • Do not use automated tools to spam submissions. You will be rate limited
  • Decisions made by the CTF organizers are final.

For questions or issues, contact the CYSCOM team.
