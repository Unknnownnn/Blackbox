About {{ ctf_name }}

What is CTF?

Capture The Flag (CTF) is a cybersecurity competition where participants solve security-related challenges to find hidden flags. These challenges test various skills including cryptography, web exploitation, reverse engineering, forensics, and more.

How to Play

  1. Register: Create an account and optionally join or create a team
  2. Browse Challenges: View available challenges in different categories
  3. Solve: Download challenge files, analyze them, and find the flag
  4. Submit: Enter the flag in the challenge submission form
  5. Score: Earn points for each correct submission. Points decrease as more teams solve the challenge

Scoring System

This CTF uses dynamic scoring. Challenge values decrease as more teams solve them:

  • Challenges start at their maximum point value
  • Points decrease logarithmically with each solve
  • Minimum points are guaranteed even with many solves
  • First blood (first solve) gets bonus recognition!

Rules

  • Do not attack the CTF infrastructure
  • Do not share flags or solutions with other teams
  • Do not use automated tools to spam submissions
  • Be respectful to other participants
  • Have fun and learn!

Contact

For questions, issues, or concerns, please contact the CTF administrators.
